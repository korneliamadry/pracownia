Our Vision

We like to share our passion - passion for learning. In our work, we value the method of communication with children. The basis for us is respect, partnership and individual approach. We focus on children's strengths, thereby strengthening their own competences.

Meetings in small groups of 6-12 people encourage activity and give space for all children to participate in the classes. Our lecturers skillfully inspire to ask questions, search for answers, hypotheses, draw conclusions.

Our Vision

402/5000 We are passionate about neurodidactics - brain-friendly teaching and learning. Neurodidactics gives evidence that the most we learn by experience. We learn most effectively by experiments.

A friendly, stress-free atmosphere is conducive to learning, where there is a place to ask questions and make mistakes. And kids learn the most when they forget that they are learning!

Our Vision

We attach great importance to safety. During classes children receive aprons and protective goggles from us. For experiments we use only safe products, which are mostly available at homes (so you can repete the experiments as well)

We work only with passionate and responsible teachers who can inspire children. All classes are conducted based on scenarios prepared by us. Each scenario was approved by a specialist with a PhD in technical sciences.
