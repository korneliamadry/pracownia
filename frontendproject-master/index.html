Our Vision

We like to share our passion - passion for learning. In our work, we value the method of communication with children. The basis for us is respect, partnership and individual approach. We focus on children's strengths, thereby strengthening their own competences.

Meetings in small groups of 6-12 people encourage activity and give space for all children to participate in the classes. Our lecturers skillfully inspire to ask questions, search for answers, hypotheses, draw conclusions.

Our Vision

402/5000 We are passionate about neurodidactics - brain-friendly teaching and learning. Neurodidactics gives evidence that the most we learn by experience. We learn most effectively by experiments.

A friendly, stress-free atmosphere is conducive to learning, where there is a place to ask questions and make mistakes. And kids learn the most when they forget that they are learning!

Our Vision

We attach great importance to safety. During classes children receive aprons and protective goggles from us. For experiments we use only safe products, which are mostly available at homes (so you can repete the experiments as well)

We work only with passionate and responsible teachers who can inspire children. All classes are conducted based on scenarios prepared by us. Each scenario was approved by a specialist with a PhD in technical sciences.

School activities

We teach children aged 4-13 during their after school time at partnering schools (check locations) all around Warsaw, Poland. We are an indipendant company that uses school facilities to broaden the vision and knowledge of children with the so called “edutainment” method. By showing them how the theory transforms and is used in real-life practical situations - how is soap made; how to grow plants; how do chemicals react to each other; how to apply maths in everyday life and many, many more!

Summer breaks

Summer breaks are something adults don’t have anymore, but children do and this is why we offer daycare during those sunny months when your child wants to play outside but you are too preoccupied with important files and deadlines at work. Let us take off some pressure from your shoulders and take your kid outside to experince nature in the best possible way - by planting trees, nurishing plants, taking care of different safe species or just play handball in the park.

Birthday parties

We believe a person should be always surrounded by people on his special day and birthdays are something children are esprecially excited about all-year-round, which is why we pepare numerous surprises to make the day unforgettable for said birthday child. Our goal is to make them happy and appreciated in every way possible and we stand strongly behind that!
